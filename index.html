About Tadas

My son Tadas was born only 28 weeks old. His weight was just 1200g (2,6lb). Tadas' condition was very serious. From the first minutes of his life he had to fight for survival. He was incubated for applying an artificial lung ventilation, because he could not breathe himself. After a few days his condition got worse. He was diagnosed with brain bruising, what resulted into hydrocephalus (the brain water).This damaged his brain. He was diagnosed with children's cerebral palsy. Doctors did not give us too much hope, they had only two opinions: my son's death or permanent disability.
Thank God, their opinions did not materialize. With the big help of professionals my son can enjoy walking, more less take care of his own life.
